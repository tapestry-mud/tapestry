# gmcp-movement
# Verifies GMCP room packets update correctly on movement.

## Scenario: Moving sends updated Room.Info and Room.Nearby
- Players: Rye

### Steps
1. Rye connects and logs in
2. Wait for post-login burst
3. Rye: `north`
4. Assert Rye receives GMCP: `Room.Info` with updated room id
5. Assert Rye receives GMCP: `Room.Nearby`

## Scenario: Moving in blocked direction sends Room.WrongDir
- Players: Rye

### Steps
1. Rye connects and logs in
2. Rye: `up` (assume no exit up in spawn room)
3. Assert Rye receives GMCP: `Room.WrongDir`
4. Assert Rye does NOT receive GMCP: `Room.Info` (room didn't change)
